What is Dark Screen?

Dark Screen is an innovative Android application that creates a realistic fake lock screen overlay, allowing you to continue running music, podcasts, or any other apps in the background while your device appears to be locked.

Whether you need to demonstrate an app without revealing your actual screen content, maintain privacy while letting media play, or simply want to prevent accidental touches during presentations, Dark Screen provides the perfect solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dark Screen

Does Dark Screen actually lock my phone?

No, Dark Screen creates an overlay that looks like a lock screen, but your phone remains fully functional underneath. You can continue to play music, run apps, and receive notifications while the fake lock screen is active.

Will Dark Screen drain my battery?

Dark Screen is optimized for minimal battery usage. The overlay is lightweight and doesn't consume significant resources. In fact, it may help save battery by preventing accidental screen touches that would otherwise wake your device.

Can I use Dark Screen with any music app?

Yes! Dark Screen works with any audio app including Spotify, YouTube Music, Apple Music, Podcast apps, and more. The fake lock screen doesn't interfere with background audio playback.

Is Dark Screen safe to use?

Absolutely. Dark Screen only uses the standard Android overlay system and doesn't require root access. It doesn't collect personal data or interfere with system operations beyond creating the visual lock screen overlay.
