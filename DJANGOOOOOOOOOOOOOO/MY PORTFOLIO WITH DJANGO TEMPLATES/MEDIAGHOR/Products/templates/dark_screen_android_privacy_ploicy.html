Privacy Policy

For Dark Screen - Privacy & Eye Comfort

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

This privacy policy describes how Media Ghor ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Android application "Dark Screen - Privacy & Eye Comfort" (the "App").

Introduction

At Media Ghor, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Dark Screen application. We are committed to being transparent about our privacy practices and ensuring your data is protected.

Dark Screen is designed to provide eye comfort and privacy by dimming your screen with a dark overlay while allowing your apps to continue running in the background. By using Dark Screen, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it to us.

No Data Sharing with Third Parties

Required Permissions

Dark Screen requires certain permissions to function properly. Here's why we need them:

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Permission

This permission allows Dark Screen to display the dark screen overlay on top of other applications for privacy and eye comfort purposes.

  • Purpose: To create the dark screen overlay for eye comfort and privacy
  • Data Accessed: None - only display capability
  • Data Stored: None

Notification Permission

This permission allows Dark Screen to show persistent notifications when the dark overlay is active, helping you manage and control the screen dimming functionality.

  • Purpose: To provide ongoing control and status of the dark screen overlay
  • Data Accessed: None - only notification display capability
  • Data Stored: None
  • Usage: Shows active overlay status and quick access to turn off the dark screen

Other Functionality Permissions

  • Internet Access: For optional crash reporting and analytics (disabled by default)
  • Foreground Service: To keep the dark screen overlay active when needed

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the Dark Screen application:

  • To provide the core functionality of displaying a dark screen overlay for eye comfort and privacy
  • To maintain app stability and prevent crashes
  • To improve user experience through optional, anonymous usage statistics
  • To respond to user support requests when voluntarily contacted

Important Note

Dark Screen does not and cannot access or view your screen content, personal data, or any sensitive information. The overlay is purely visual and does not have the capability to interact with or capture what's displayed beneath it. The app is designed solely for providing screen dimming functionality for eye comfort and privacy.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal process
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app (e.g., crash reporting services), subject to strict data protection obligations
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

No Advertising Networks

Dark Screen does not integrate with any advertising networks and does not display third-party advertisements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we process. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our Security Measures

  • Minimal data collection principle
  • Local processing of information where possible
  • Regular security reviews of our code and infrastructure
  • Use of secure communication protocols when data transmission is necessary

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing

Practical Actions

  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Disable optional analytics in app settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
  • Revoke app permissions via device settings

For EU Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we collect minimal personal data, most of these rights don't apply, but we're committed to honoring them where relevant.

For California Users (CCPA)

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides California residents with specific rights regarding their personal information. As we don't sell personal data and collect minimal information, many CCPA provisions don't apply to our app.

Children's Privacy

Dark Screen is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

COPPA Compliance

Our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not specifically market to children under 13, and our data collection practices are designed to minimize any potential impact on children's privacy.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

Since we collect minimal data and primarily process it locally on your device, international data transfers are limited. Any data that is transferred internationally (such as optional crash reports) is protected through appropriate safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Notification of Material Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will provide a more prominent notice, such as an in-app notification or email (if you've provided your contact information).
