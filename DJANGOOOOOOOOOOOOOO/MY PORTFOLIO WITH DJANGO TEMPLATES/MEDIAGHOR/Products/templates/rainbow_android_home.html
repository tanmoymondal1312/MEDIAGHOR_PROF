Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Rainbow Tools

How accurate is the background removal feature?

Our AI-powered background removal is highly accurate for most images. It works best with clear subjects and contrasting backgrounds. For complex images, we provide manual editing tools to refine the results.

Does Rainbow Tools work offline?

Some basic functions like simple image adjustments work offline, but advanced features like AI background removal, text extraction, and format conversion require an internet connection to process files on our secure servers.

What file formats does Rainbow Tools support?

We support all major image formats (JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF) and document formats (PDF, DOC, TXT). Our conversion tools allow you to transform between these formats while maintaining quality.

Is my data safe with Rainbow Tools?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Files processed through our servers are automatically deleted after processing. We don't store your personal data or images, and all transmissions are encrypted.
